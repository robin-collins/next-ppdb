# Features Overview

Next PPDB provides a comprehensive set of features for managing your pet grooming business. This section covers all major functionality.

## Core Features

### Search

Quickly find animals, customers, and breeds across the entire database.

- [Search](/docs/features/search) - Smart search with relevance ranking and flexible phone matching

### Animal Management

View and manage individual animal records and grooming history.

- [Animal Details](/docs/features/animals) - Animal profiles, service history, and notes

### Customer Management

Track customer information, contact details, and their associated pets.

- [Customer Management](/docs/features/customers) - Customer profiles and history

### Breed Database

Maintain a database of breeds with pricing and time estimates.

- [Breeds](/docs/features/breeds) - Breed management and pricing

### Reporting

Generate reports for business insights and daily operations.

- [Reports](/docs/features/reports) - Daily totals and customer analytics

### Administration

System administration and data management tools.

- [Admin Tools](/docs/features/admin) - Backup and restore functionality
